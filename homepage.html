The University of Sugar Land

The University of Sugar Land is an additional site under the University of Houston brand. This space is located in Sugar Land's Fort Bend ISD. It can be found at the corner of US-59/69 and University Blvd. This part of campus offers programs for both undergraduates and graduates from UH Colleges of Nursing, Engineering, Education, Social Work, Business and Liberal Arts & Social Sciences.


For additional information, please check out our main page that provides all the information about what the Sugar Land campus can offer you and/or your student.


What Is Cafe Ease?

Cafe Ease was a cafe created by Madison Young to ensure students were able to find a place to study in peace. While most establishes focus on the environment and socialization aspect, Cafe Ease was created to ensure socialization was not an expectation for students who may want a quieter dining experience. Made similar to traditional cafes, tables and a counter space are offered to customers who want a typical experience, but on the second floor is separated into corrals for customers to find peace with themselves, set up in a similar fashion to Japan's restuarants where little interaction is necessary.
